Base_tavern- Guild info - Tavern Game
name: Dice Forge: The Battle of Numbers
rules: Dice Forge: The Battle of Numbers is a game of strategy and luck played with ten d6 dice. Each player takes on the role of a powerful sorcerer racing to harness the power of a mystical forge. On their turn, a player rolls all ten dice and tries to get as many of them to show the same number as possible. They can keep any dice that match and re-roll the rest, trying to build a set of ten matching dice as quickly as possible. Once a player has ten matching dice, the game ends and points are tallied.
goal: The goal of Dice Forge: The Battle of Numbers is to be the player with the most points at the end of the game, which is triggered when a player manages to build a set of ten matching dice. Players earn points based on the total value of their dice, but only the matches count towards the loser's score. Players must use strategy and luck to build their set of matching dice as quickly as possible while also trying to prevent their opponents from doing the same.
