Monster- Description - Possible scenes
Daytime description: In the harsh light of day, a colossal figure can be seen lumbering through the forest. Its rough, earth-toned skin is marked by old scars and its ragged clothing flutters with each powerful stride. It carries a greatclub in one hand, and a rock in the other, ready for any threats.
Nighttime description: In the shadowy veil of the night, a hulking silhouette can be seen moving among the trees. The moonlight glints off the bone necklace around its neck, and the rough texture of its skin is just visible. The giant's large club and rock are ready to fend off nocturnal predators.
Attack description: With a deafening roar, the giant bursts from its hiding place, greatclub swinging and rock soaring through the air. Its massive frame shakes the ground with each step, and its rough, earth-toned skin seems to blend with the surrounding terrain.
